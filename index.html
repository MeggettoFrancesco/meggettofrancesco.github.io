I am Francesco, a final year PhD Candidate at the University of Strathclyde in the Computer & Information Sciences Department.

About me

My research focuses on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Information Retrieval. More specifically, my focus is on understanding and modelling users' behaviour in the music and podcast domain. I am supervised by Dr Yashar Moshfeghi and Professor Crawford Revie.

My primary research interests are:
  • Recommender Systems
  • (Deep) Reinforcement Learning
  • Information Retrieval

For a list of my publications, please check out my Google Scholar profile.
